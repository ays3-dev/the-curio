F.A.Q

Why use The Curio?

Standard algorithms prioritize SEO. We prioritize the obscure, the rare, and the authentic connections between topics.

Is this just another search wrapper?

No. The Curio uses a custom-built relational logic engine. It doesn't just find links; it builds a visual map of how ideas, artifacts, and histories are secretly intertwined.

How do I start a "Dig"?

Simply type any topic on the homepage. Our custom layout engine handles the cross-referencing for you.
